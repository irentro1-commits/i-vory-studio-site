Full management packages

If you want to stop thinking about social media and hand off the whole pipeline—creation, scheduling, posting—you need a management retainer. Means: content produced, scheduled, and published daily across all platforms.

Full management rates in Romania range from €400 to €3,000/month, driven by volume and complexity. Our rates start at €600/month for daily posting across 4 platforms.

What a management package includes

A solid management package covers: content creation (posts, stories, videos), scheduling and cross-platform publishing, editorial calendar, consulting. Higher-tier agencies add performance reporting, trend tracking, and strategy.

At i-vory Studio, all packages include full management with scheduling, editorial calendar, and consulting. Larger packages also include trend tracking, performance reports, and strategy.

Agency vs. DIY: which is worth it

Time decides. If you have 2-3 hours/day for social media, DIY works. If you run a business, you don't have those hours. A €600/month package frees up the time you'd otherwise burn on content production.

Math: if your hour is worth more than €3, a management package pays for itself.

Romania's social media market growth in 2024

Digital advertising in Romania reached €283-298 million in 2024, up 15.6% vs. 2023 (IAB Europe AdEx 2024). Total media spend including TV and print hit €778-795 million, per Initiative Media's Media Fact Book 2024.

Social media took the biggest share. Statista Market Insights estimates Romanian social ad spend at ~$280 million in 2024, with an 11.2% annual growth rate through 2028.

Translation for business owners: budgets are shifting to social, attention competition is rising. An SMB absent now loses ground to active competitors.

Romania-specific signal: MOCAPP reports 22-30% of Romanian digital budget goes to influencer marketing, vs. 4-7% across the rest of Europe. Local audiences buy on human recommendation over classic advertising.

What you pay for social media management in 2026

Public rates in the Romanian market fall into three tiers, with wide gaps between them.

Individual freelancer: €300-500/month for 8-12 posts, no paid campaigns. Entry level, suitable for testing or small launches (public rates on Brand Mastery).

Small-to-mid agency: €700-1,500/month for 12-20 posts, monthly reporting, campaign coordination. Standard tier for active SMBs (NION Communication publishes €450/900/1,800 packages).

Premium specialized agency: €2,000-5,000/month, with strategy, photo/video production, paid campaign management, analytics. Tier for companies above €400k revenue targeting measurable results, not decorative presence.

Important: these are agency-reported rates, not client-paid rates. Final pricing depends on industry complexity, production volume, and concrete objectives.

No public report consolidates average SMB social media management spend in Romania. A business owner negotiating should request 2-3 comparable quotes for a real baseline.
